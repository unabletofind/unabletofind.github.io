About Me

I'm a SOC Analyst passionate about improving threat detection quality and operational efficiency. I specialize in correlating Event Logs, Sysmon, and network traffic (Zeek/Wireshark) to identify lateral movement, privilege escalation, persistence mechanisms, and data exfiltration attempts.


My approach focuses on tuning detection rules, enriching alerts with context, and building analyst-friendly playbooks. I map incidents to the MITRE ATT&CK framework, analyze malware samples, and develop YARA and Sigma rules to strengthen defensive capabilities.
